# Prompt research hypothesis backlog

Test one item at a time. Add hypotheses here only from observed failure patterns,
not from hidden holdout wording.

## Ready

1. **Causal evidence bridge** — Require each major project claim to state what
   the evidence changed in the writer's question, method, or next decision.
2. **Uncertainty preservation** — Make calibrated uncertainty an explicit voice
   invariant so the rewrite does not convert exploration into false certainty.
3. **Programme-fit placeholder threshold** — Prefer a concise `[ADD: ...]`
   placeholder over generic praise when no programme-specific feature is
   supplied.
4. **Anti-resume paragraph test** — Require every body paragraph to contain
   interpretation, not only credentials or chronology.
5. **Plain-language final audit** — Remove prestige-heavy and stock application
   phrases when a direct equivalent preserves meaning.
6. **Conflict visibility** — Explicitly surface contradictory source facts
   instead of silently selecting one.

## Later, after prompt experiments plateau

- Compare one versus two constrained revision passes.
- Compare stage model routing under the same cost cap.
- Calibrate deterministic quality thresholds against blind-judge outcomes.

These later items require changes outside the prompt allowlist and therefore
must use a separately reviewed research programme.
